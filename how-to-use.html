HOW TO USE

Step 1: Select your section

First, you need to know what team you are in. Either the misinformation team, the Online Saftey team, the 2 Factor Authentication team, or the Search History team. Once you know your team, you can proceed to the next step.

Step 2: Go to your team's page

By default when you go to the main page, the sections will not load by itself. You need to specify a section so the website knows what question to display.
The site has already been preconfigured with all the questions loaded up for each section. Click on the link to your section to load it up (ctrl click to open in a new tab):

  • Misinformation
  • Online Safety
  • 2 Factor Authentication
  • Search History

Step 3: Set up sound (optional)

This is optional, but you can turn on sound with the little sound button in the bottom right of the screen. Only do this if you're in an area where playing the same sound again and again isn't going to get anoyying to people around you.
